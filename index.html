About BELLA~B

Denver Roots, Austin Expansion

BELLA~B moves like she’s already booked — because she is. What started in Denver’s late-night circuit turned into a rapid climb built on packed rooms, repeat sets, and a reputation for delivering exactly what the floor needs.


Now sharing energy between Denver and Austin, she’s pushing outward — tighter sets, heavier pressure, bigger rooms. No overthinking, no filler. Just movement, connection, and a sound that keeps people locked in longer than they planned.

BELLA~B — Denver-rooted, Austin-active. Built for momentum.
